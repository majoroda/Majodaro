Our Strategy

Majodaro Investments Holdings LLC is a family office, funded entirely by its private family capital. We invest in businesses and real estate opportunities we believe in.

When we add to our portfolio, we’re seeking long-term opportunities to maximize the potential of a company or property with a solid value proposition and untapped growth. Since all of our capital is our family’s, we are not bound by investment time horizons or mandated exits.
