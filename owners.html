Our Commitment to Business Owners

Enterprenuerial Stewardship

We understand selling a business is one of the largest financial decisions in an entrepreneur's life and that great emotional weight comes with such a decision. The importance of choosing the right group to steward and build upon what they have created is one of the biggest decisions they will make.

When we make an investment in a company, it’s also a commitment from our team to work tirelessly to continue the growth and development of that business.

Our Commitment

Majoroda Investments Holdings LLC is committed to working closely with business owners to work through each step together, making the transition as smooth as possible. This is a time when owners are able to unlock the tremendous value that has been created by their business

Majoroda Investments Holdings LLC is not private equity or a traditional family office. It is our goal to maintain and build upon the legacy that has been established while taking the company to the next level of sustainable growth. We are not looking to quickly flip a business. This gives us the ability to offer unique advantages to business owners and arrange a customized solution meeting the seller's objectives.

What We Provide


INTEGRITY

Simply put, we believe that we should leave your business better off than when we bought it. We will stay true to our values of transparency, honesty, dedication, and hard work.


LEGACY PRESERVATION

It is important to us that we conserve the legacy of the company and build upon its reputation. We seek to acquire companies with incredible value propositions and strong growth potential. Furthermore, employees of our acquisitions keep their jobs and our provided further opportunity to reach their full potential. We’re not looking to fix what isn’t broken - but instead, to continue growing the top line and selectively improving operations


FLEXIBLE STRUCTURE

With a long-term focus and our private family funding, we are not bound by a rigid timeframe to return capital, allowing us to focus and invest for the long-term success of the company. This gives us the ability to be flexible in tailoring the terms of any deal to meet the owner's unique needs and objectives.


FOCUS

We are not managing a large group of portfolio companies. This allows us to put all of our energy and focus on a small number of companies’ growth and success. We are committed to the long-term success of each business we own
